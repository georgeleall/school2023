Lesson Notes

Lesson 28: Organizing and Managing a Website

Summary:

In this lesson, we learned the importance of not putting way too many items on your website. If your website is stacked, nobody will read everything. You would want to keep your website as simple as possible.

Having a simple website will be able to hold the attention of users for far longer than a website stacked with information will. If you also overstack your website it won't look nice. Keeping your site simple is beneficial to everyone.

What You'll Learn:

  • How to determine whether one page is enough to handle all your content
  • How to organize a simple site
  • How to organize a larger site
  • How to write maintainable code
  • How to optimize your site for search engines
  • How to get started with version control

Reflection on the Lesson:

I believe keeping a simple site, making it look nice, and not overstacking your website is the best thing you can do. This is because it will catch the attention of new users and hold the attention of current users. If you have an ugly website and too much information, no one will look at or read the information you are trying to spread.
